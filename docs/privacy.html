WattPulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

WattPulse ("we", "us", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use WattPulse.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. WattPulse is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • Home Assistant Connection Data: Your HA URL and Long-Lived Access Token are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device only. They are never transmitted to us.
  • Energy Usage Data: All energy records, daily summaries, and cost calculations are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. They never leave your device.
  • App Settings: Rate plans, notification preferences, and appearance settings are stored in iOS UserDefaults on your device.
  • Feedback Messages: If you voluntarily contact support through the in-app form, your name, email, and message are sent to our feedback service to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display real-time and historical energy data from your Home Assistant instance.
  • To calculate costs, savings, and CO₂ offset locally on your device.
  • To generate AI-powered suggestions entirely on-device.
  • To respond to your support inquiries when you contact us.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Keychain: Access tokens are encrypted and stored in iOS Keychain.
  • SwiftData: Energy data is stored in an encrypted local database.
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data is never synced to iCloud or any cloud service.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics SDKs or tracking libraries.

4. Data Retention

Energy data is retained on your device for up to 2 years (730 days). Older data is automatically deleted. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Home Assistant: WattPulse connects directly to your Home Assistant instance over your local network or VPN. Home Assistant's privacy policy applies to data on your HA server.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used for one-time purchase verification. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • We do not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or third-party trackers.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored on your device through the app's CSV export feature.
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app.
  • Revoke Home Assistant access tokens at any time from your HA profile.
  • Disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings or in-app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

WattPulse does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
